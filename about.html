Why AmaRNA?

You're probably curious about our name, so let's talk about its origins. The name 'AmaRNA' is a portmanteau of the Latin word amare, meaning 'love', and RNA, one of the most important molecules in biology, and we think this really captures the essence of our organization. As we were brainstorming different names, we chose 'AmaRNA' because it embodies our commitment to healing patients, both through advancing the field of medicine and caring for patients as our fellow humans.

Our Mission

Here at Project AmaRNA, we strive to help patients by making information about medicine and about health conditions accessible and easy to understand. We want to make healthcare feel more comfortable for people by helping them to understand what is happening to their bodies and how medicine works.

Who We Are

We are a group of Stanford students, connected by the unifying desire to help others. Since most of us are pre-med students, we will be both learning about and conducting research in the medical field, and we look forward to bringing this information to you.
