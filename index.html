Peers Over Profit

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, Peerlift is completely free.

We connect high school students of all backgrounds to a proven selection of exceptional opportunities for college.

Student-Driven

In our 20-person team,

100%

are 18 years old or younger.

Peerlift is the only major opportunities platform exclusively run by current students.

A Unique Approach

We offer a curated set of 350 opportunities. All offer financial aid.

No more searching through millions of unhelpful scholarships. We focus on helping low- and middle- income students find resources for success.

Proven Content

Completely crowdsourced.

College freshmen from across the country shared what made higher education a reality for them. Each opportunity you see was invaluable to another student like you.
